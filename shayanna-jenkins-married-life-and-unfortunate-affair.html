Shayanna Jenkins' Married Life and Unfortunate Affair!

Publish date: 2024-07-17

Bearing the death of closed ones can be the hardest thing for anyone, especially when it is a suicide. Shayanna Jenkins lost a part of her world when she lost her fiance, Aaron Hernandez back in April 2017.

The lover experienced a horrific tragedy when she was only 28. Later on, the diva even featured on a Netflix documentary regarding the case and hidden mystery behind her beau’s demise. So, today we take you close to the personal life, romantic affairs and her perspective into Hernandez’s suicidal case.

Shayanna Jenkins Engaged to Late Aaron Hernandez

The dashing diva, Shayanna Jenkins rose to prominence after dating the NFL star, Aaron Hernandez. The star player from new England Patriots met with her in their elementary school and started seeing each other from high school.

The school-level lovers were all set to take their relationship to the next level by getting married. Thus, the footballer got on one knee and proposed the American for a marriage in 2012. It was a dream come true for Shayanna and undoubtedly she said yes.

Jenkins and Hernandez Were Parents to One

The charming beauty, Shayanna Jenkins and Aaron Hernandez even shared an adorable daughter. A few months before they got married, the high school lovers welcomed their angel, named Avielle Janelle Hernandez back on November 6, 2012

During their early days with their daughter, Jenkins accused fiance Hernandez of cheating with their nanny. However, they worked the problem between themselves later on.

Jenkins’s Fiance Hernandez Committed Suicide

Jenkins’s fiance, Aaron Hernandez never failed to be on the media stories, be it with his football performances or controversies. In 2013, he faced a first-degree murder charge of his brother in law, Odin Lloyd. Shockingly, the court found him guilty and sentenced him to life imprisonment.

Once a football sensation, Hernandez committed suicide in the prison on 19 April 2017. He hanged himself by his bedsheets to the jail’s window to end his life.

Shayanna Jenkins in Netflix’s Documentary

The heart-broken lover, Shayana Jenkins featured in a Netflix documentary, Killer Inside: The Mind of Aaron Hernandez in 2020. Interestingly, the movie unveils the personality of the late footballer and the details that led to the death of Jenkins’s former lover.

Shayanna Jenkins Moved on and is Married

About almost a year after Hernandez’s shocking demise, Shayanna Jenkins moved on with her life. Today, she is happily married to former boxer Dino Guilmette. Besides, the Instagram personality welcomed her second daughter, this time with Dino in the fall of 2018.

After her tragedy with Hernandez, Jenkins relocated to Providence, Rhode Island, where the next chapter of her life began. Interestingly, she met the boxer in the same city and they started to date.
