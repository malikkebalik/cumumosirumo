Mark Hite Obituary, Chattanooga Realtor, Philanthropist Mark Hite Passes Away At Age 58

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Mark Hite, a top-selling real estate agent in Chattanooga and a patron of numerous community NGOs, was discovered deceased in Key West, Florida, on Tuesday, as per the police report. He was fifty-eight.

A police spokeswoman stated that Hite was declared deceased at the scene and taken to the medical examiner after Key West police responded to a call for a welfare check in the 400 block of Emma Street at around 2:00 p.m. on Tuesday.

“Until the autopsy report is received, there is no evidence of foul play,” an email from Key West police spokesman Alyson Crean stated.

Hite owned a condo on Emma Street, according to property records in Monroe County, Florida. Colleagues reported he had been on vacation in Key West.

After spending 13 years with Keller William Realty, Hite—who was born and raised in Kevil, Kentucky—built a team of over twenty agents and worked in the Chattanooga real estate market for almost 22 years. In 2020, the Mark Hite team joined Chattanooga Real Estate Partners.

Regarding the post, Darlene Brown writes:

“As many of you have heard, we have all experienced a great loss. Mark Hite passed away this weekend in Key West.
“His passing leaves us heartbroken. It’s one that will be felt deeply within our company, across our real estate industry, and throughout our city for a long time.
We will keep you apprised of plans for remembrance as we learn more. To Mark’s clients, please direct any inquiries to our Downtown Office at 423.265.0088”.

“In the meantime, I am reminded of Mark’s unwavering standard of excellence and desire to make a meaningful impact where it was needed most. I hope we all enter into 2024 with a similar mindset to not waste one day we are given. It is what Mark would want”.
