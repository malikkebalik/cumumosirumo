Johnny Flynn is an English musician, singer-songwriter, and actor best known as the lead singer and songwriter of the band Johnny Flynn & The Sussex Wit. Similarly, he is well-known for his role as Dylan Witter in the Netflix sitcom Lovesick.

How much is the Net Worth of Johnny Flynn?

Johnny Flynn, the singer-actor has appeared in a number of films and television shows. Similarly, he and his band have released a total of seven studio albums. According to various sources, Johnny’s net worth is estimated to be around $1 million as of 2022. He has over 24.2k Instagram followers and over 29.8k Twitter followers.

Early Life and Education

On March 14, 1983, in Johannensburg, South Africa, John Patrick Vivian Flynn was born to British singer and actor Eric Flynn and Caroline Forbes. He will be 38 years old in 2021. When Flynn was two years old, his family relocated to the United Kingdom. Similarly, he grew up with half-siblings Jerome Flynn and Daniel Flynn (both actors), Kerry Flynn, and Lillie Flynn.

He attended Pilgrims School, where he received a music scholarship, for his education. Flynn then attended Bedales School, where he received a second music scholarship. He later attended the Webber Douglas Academy of Dramatic Art, where he studied acting.

Johnny Flynn Makes His Acting Debut

Flynn made his acting debut in the British TV series Murder in Suburbia in 2005 as Josh Egan. He then played Karl Massingham in Holby City and Dolf Vega in Crusade in Jeans. On May 26, 2008, he released his debut album, A Larum, in the United Kingdom, and on July 29, 2008, in the United States. Hit singles from the album include The Box, The Wrote & The Writ, Tickle Me Pink, and others.

On June 7, 2010, Johnny and the band released their second full-length album Been Listening, which included the singles Kentucky Pill, Los and Found, The Water, and The Prizefighter. Transgressive released their third album, A Film Score of a Bag of Hammers, in 2012. I Don’t Wanna Go, Bikes, I Am Light, and Where My Father Went are among the songs on the album.

Johnny played Dylan Witter in the Channel 4 and Netflix sitcom Lovesick from 2014 to 2018. He co-stars in the show with Antonia Thomas, Daniel Ings, Hannah Birtland, and Joshia McGuire. In 2017, he co-starred with Geoffrey Rush, Samantha Colley, Emily Watson, and Ralph Brown in the American anthology period drama series Genius as Young Albert Einstein. Flynn was nominated for a Critics’ Choice Television Award for his outstanding performance. Other films in which he has appeared include Beast, Emma, Stardust, and The Dig. Likewise, his upcoming film projects include Operation Mincemeat and The Score.

Married to a childhood sweetheart

The Lovesick actor is married to his long-term girlfriend, theatre designer Beatrice Minns. The couple started dating in high school and had an on-again, off-again relationship.

They married in 2011 and have three children, Gabriel, Ida, and Lorca.
