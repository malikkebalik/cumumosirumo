Who Is Ankita Lokhande? - Ankita Lokhande Biography

Ankita Lokhande Biography:- Ankita Lokhande is an Indian actress. She was born on 19 December 1984 in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. She rose to fame after appearing in Pavitra Rishta, a TV show which was aired on ZeeTv. She has received many awards including three times Gold Awards, the Indian Television Academy Award, and the Indian Telly Award. She then appeared in the dance reality show Jhalak Dikhlaja 4. She made her film debut with Manikarnika: The Queen of Jhansi as Jhalkaribai in 2019.

After then in 2020 she appeared in the movie Baaghi 3 with Tiger Shroff, Shraddha Kapoor, and Riteish Deshmukh. In 2021 she appeared in Pavitra Rishta: It's Never Too Late as Archana Deshmukh. She and her spouse are competitors in the reality TV series Bigg Boss 17 as of October 2023.
