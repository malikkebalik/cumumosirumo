The Meaning Behind The Song: “Getting Drippy” by Matt Shatt & BigLad

TitleGetting Drippy
ArtistMatt Shatt & BigLad
Writer/ComposerMatt Shatt & BigLad
AlbumNot specified
Release DateJune 12, 2022
GenreRap
ProducerBigLad Beats

When it comes to music, every song carries a unique meaning that resonates with different individuals in different ways. “Getting Drippy” by Matt Shatt & BigLad is no exception. With its catchy lyrics and infectious beats, this rap track offers listeners a chance to dive into the world of self-expression and personal style.

The song opens with the line “I’m getting drippy, Oh I’m getting drippy.” This repetitive phrase sets the tone for the entire track, portraying the artists’ pride and enthusiasm in their own fashion choices. The term “drippy” is often used in pop culture to describe someone who has an impeccable sense of style and exudes confidence through their unique clothing and accessories.

In the first verse, Matt Shatt expresses his excitement about his drip (fashion style) and how it brings him joy. He emphasizes that his choice of drip is crucial and meaningful to him, as it represents his triumphant emergence from a difficult period in his life. The line “From death I arose” suggests that he has overcome personal challenges and found solace in his choice of fashion.

The second verse takes a competitive turn, hinting at a rap battle with an unnamed individual named Jimmy. Matt Shatt asserts that only BigLad, his collaborator, can match his level of drip. By dismissing others’ fashion choices, he playfully highlights his own superior style. This verse adds a touch of humor and lightheartedness to the song, showcasing the artists’ confidence and camaraderie.

While the song appears to be focused on fashion and style, the lyrics in the third verse hint at a deeper emotion. Matt Shatt describes feeling “blue” but clarifies that it is not a reflection of his personal state. Instead, he attributes it to the way others dress, suggesting that their fashion sense disappoints or upsets him. This verse opens up a discussion about judgment and the impact of appearances, shedding light on how personal style can evoke strong reactions and emotions.

The bridge of the song is a playful, repetitive segment filled with catchy vibes and wordplay. It further emphasizes the fun and light-hearted nature of “Getting Drippy.” The artists use sounds and rhythm to keep the energy high, capturing the listeners’ attention and providing a catchy hook that resonates throughout the song.

Personal experience with this song:

When I first heard “Getting Drippy,” I couldn’t help but nod my head and tap my feet to the infectious beat. The song’s exciting energy and playful lyrics made it an instant favorite of mine. While I may not be a rap aficionado, I found myself drawn to the sentiment behind the song.

As someone who believes in the power of personal expression through fashion, “Getting Drippy” spoke to me on a deeper level. The idea of self-confidence and joy found in one’s unique style resonated with my own experiences. I have always believed that fashion is a form of self-expression, allowing individuals to showcase their personalities and creativity.

This song reminded me of how fashion can be a source of empowerment, especially during challenging times. Just like Matt Shatt and BigLad, we all face obstacles and difficulties in life. However, our choice of clothing and accessories can act as a personal armor, boosting our confidence and helping us rise above adversity.

Overall, “Getting Drippy” is more than just a catchy rap track. It’s a celebration of personal style, resilience, and the power of self-expression. Matt Shatt and BigLad deliver a fun and energetic performance that invites listeners to embrace their own unique fashion choices, regardless of societal norms or judgments.

So, the next time you find yourself getting dressed in the morning, remember the message behind “Getting Drippy” and let your fashion choices empower you. Express yourself through your style and embrace the drippy side of life!
