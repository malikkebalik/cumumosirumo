Jeannie Mai is an American Television personality, make-up artist, and a fashion expert. Mai is popular as the host of the Style Network's makeover show How do I Look? Jeannie also runs the You-Tube channel, a program named Hello Hunnay with Jeannie Mai.

Jeannie Camtu Mai was born on January 4, 1979, in San Jose, California, USA. She was born to a Vietnamese mother and a Chinese father. Her mother is identified by her social media name The Mama Mai. Jeannie has a brother named Daniel Mai. She attended Milpitas High School.

Relationship Status: Engaged

The 41-years-old TV host Jeannie is in a romantic relationship with Jay Wayne Jenkins, aka Jeezy. Jeannie's love partner Jeezy is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and philanthropist. Jeezy has released the songs like And Then What, Soul Survivor, Lose My Mind, Seen It All, My President, among many others.

As per People, Jeezy was planning to propose her during the trip to Vietnam. But due to the global pandemic of COVID 19, their trip got canceled and his decision to propose her as well. As of now, the newly engaged pair are living in a home quarantine together sharing a love. She also never misses a chance to flaunt romantic pictures on her social accounts.
