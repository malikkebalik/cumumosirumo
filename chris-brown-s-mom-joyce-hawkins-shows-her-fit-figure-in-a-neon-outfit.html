Chris Brown's Mom Joyce Hawkins Shows Her Fit Figure in a Neon Outfit

Publish date: 2024-09-10

Chris Brown’s mother, Joyce Hawkins, showed off her trim figure in a post she recently dropped on her Instagram page.

Over the last few years, Chris Brown’s mother, Joyce Hawkins, has become a well-known and very present figure — both in the singer-songwriter’s life and on social media.

Joyce Hawkins and her son, Chris Brown, arrive at Chris Brown's Birthday Bash at Avalon May 06, 2007 in New York City | Photo: Getty Images

The mother of two increased her social media presence when she took to her Instagram feed to post a picture that featured her leaning against a car.

The “New Flames” hitmaker allegedly threw a rock at [his mother] Joyce’s car window following a heated argument between them.

While the car caught people’s attention, the highlight of the image was Joyce’s outfit, which was a combination of a white top and neon green shorts.

Joyce finished off the look with a pair of green neon sneakers and a hand on her hip. The post, which has garnered nearly 10,000 likes, also got her followers talking in the comment section.

This wouldn’t be the first time that the mother of two’s outfit would attract attention. Back in January 2019, Hot New Hip Hop reported that she uploaded a look from one of her photoshoots to Instagram.

The shoot saw Joyce rocking a pair of thigh-high boots that she complemented with a black sheet top and jean shorts. Besides getting accolades for her glam looks, Joyce also got applauded when she posted a throwback image of her famous son.

According to HollywoodLife, the throwback pic featured a teenage Chris wearing a polo shirt, a necklace, and a baseball cap. The mother of two used the caption to exclaim that Royalty, Chris’ first child, is the replica of her now-30-year-old dad.

Less than a month after that post, HollywoodLife disclosed that Joyce shared an image of her second grandchild from Chris, Aeko Catori Brown, with the caption:

“WELL WELL LOOK WHO POPPED UP IN MY PHONE TODAY!!! MY OTHER ANGEL!!!”

It would appear that Joyce has come a long way since 2013 when she expressed some concerns about her son’s friends.

Back then, Daily Mail divulged that Hawkins spoke out after fans began speculating about an incident that occurred between her and Chris. The “New Flames” hitmaker allegedly threw a rock at Joyce’s car window following a heated argument between them.

The outlet claimed that “Mom Breezy,” as she is fondly referred to, was worried that her son was mixing up with the “wrong crowd.” That fear, however, didn’t stop her from publicly showing her support for him during his past domestic and legal issues.
