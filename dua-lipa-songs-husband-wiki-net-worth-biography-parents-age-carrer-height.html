Dua Lipa Early life and Career

Dua Lipa was born on 22 August 1995 in London England. Her father's name is Dukagjin Lipa and her mother's name is Anesa Lipa. She has one sister, Rina Lipa and one brother Gjin Lipa. She is the eldest child of Kosovo Albanian parents. She belongs to Muslim family but through her maternal grandmother, she is of Bosnian descent. She was inspired in music by her father. She began singing at an early age of five years. She raised in West Hampstead, London. She educated from Fitzjohn's Primary School.

She started her career by covering songs such as "If I Ain't Got You" and "Beautiful" on You Tube. Lipa signed with a modelling agency that gave her a role as a "singer" in an ITV advertisement for The X Factor in 2013, covering the song "Lost in Music".Lipa released her first single, "New Love",  in August 2015.
