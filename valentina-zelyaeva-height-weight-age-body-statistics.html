Valentina Zelyaeva Facts

  • She loves yoga, photography, nature, animals, and traveling.
  • Valentina is good friends with Flavia de Oliveira, Filippa Hamilton, Katia Kokoreva, Natasha Poly, and Ginta Lapina.
  • She moved to Moscow (in Russia) at the age of 7 with her family.
  • As a child, she wanted to become a flight attendant so that she can travel the world.
  • When she was 16, she was taken to Elite Model Look contest by her friends. She didn’t win it. Instead, her two friends won. But, she met with a scouting agent, who sent her to Tokyo (Japan) for two months. Hence, her career kick-started.
  • In 2005, she signed a contract with Ralph Lauren.
  • She likes to read inspirational books, search for a healthy lifestyle on the internet, ski, wake-boarding, capoeira, cooking in her free time.
  • She writes for organiceyourlife.com.
