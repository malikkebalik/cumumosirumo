Who is Geri Halliwell?

Geri Halliwell Wiki: - Geri Halliwell's full name is Geraldine Estelle Halliwell Horner. She is a British singer, songwriter, author, and actress. She was born on 6 August 1972 at Watford General Hospital in Watford, Hertfordshire. She is renowned as a former member of the immensely popular British girl group, the Spice Girls. She was previously known as "Ginger Spice" during her time with the group.

The Spice Girls became a global phenomenon in the late 1990s which included members Mel B (Scary Spice), Emma Bunton (Baby Spice), Melanie C (Sporty Spice), and Victoria Beckham (Posh Spice), with hit songs like "Wannabe," "Spice Up Your Life," and "Say You'll Be There." She left the Spice Girls in 1998 to pursue a solo career. She released albums such as "Schizophonic" and "Scream If You Wanna Go Faster." Later, she reunited with the Spice Girls for a reunion tour in 2007 and another one in 2019. Geri Halliwell has also been involved in television, writing children's books, and working as a judge on TV talent shows along with her music career.
