Nikki Phillips Welcomes Third Child with Husband Dane Rumble

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Nikki Phillips already had an enviable family life with her husband Dane Rumble and two children — Jett and Luca. And now, the family of four has become a family of five.

Phillips gave birth to her third child, a daughter, on March 16, 2022. She recently took to social media to share her happiness with the world.

Nikki Phillips Gives Birth to Third Child

Phillips took to Instagram on March 21, 2022, to reveal details of her daughter's birth.

The model uploaded a few adorable pictures of her daughter for the announcement post. The first two were of just her sleeping with baby bandanas on.

Nikki Phillips's two sons looking elated after she welcomed her third child, daughter Billie Rose Rumble, in March 2022. (Photo: Nikki Phillips/Instagram)

The third picture showed her placed between her two sons, who looked absolutely delighted to have gotten a baby sister.

It might have been a more than perfect birthday gift for Luca, who celebrated his third year around the sun on March 19.

The internet was then filled with people looking up— "daughter name" to figure out her name.

"Billie Rose Rumble," she wrote at the start of the caption, revealing her daughter's name.

She then welcomed her little princess into "this crazy world" and said the whole family was "besotted" with her.

Ending the caption, she wrote "16.03.22," the birth date of her baby girl.

The announcement post was flooded with congratulatory comments from her followers and famous friends.

Supermodel Megan Gale wrote, "Congratulations lovely!" and Rebecca Judd chimed in, saying, "Awwww! Welcome, Billie."

Phillips first announced her third pregnancy in November 2021.

"INCOMING THE THIRD AMIGO. Excited to announce we have one in the oven," she wrote in her post on November 3, 2021, while showing her positive pregnancy test to the camera.

Nikki Phillips's Family of Five

Phillips's happy family life started after a horrific health scare for her. In 2009, she was diagnosed with cervical cancer after a regular pap smear came back iffy.

After this, she took a break from modeling to recover, and only after her recovery did she meet her husband, Rumble.

It took three years and a supportive boyfriend for her to be declared cancer-free, but in November 2012, she achieved that. She expressed her wish to have a family with Rumble at the time. But he was fine if she could not have kids.

About a year later, in June 2013, the couple got engaged. He popped the question during their trip to Hong Kong.

Then came the wedding.

The Sydney-based socialite married her musician partner on May 27, 2014. Their wedding was a beautiful ceremony held in Bali with her close friends and family in attendance.

They had tried having babies but failed because of her three miscarriages. Then, a miracle happened in 2017.

She fell pregnant with her first kid, son Jett, that year and gave birth to him on July 17, 2018.

On March 19, 2019, their second son, Luca Fox, came into the earth.

March sure is going to be a busy month for the Phillips family now, with two birthdays to celebrate just days apart.
