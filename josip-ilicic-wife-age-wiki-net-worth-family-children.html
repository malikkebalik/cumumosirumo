Josip Ilicic Wikipedia

Josip Ilicic Wikipedia: - Josip Ilicic was born on 29 January 1988 in Prijedor, SR Bosnia and Herzegovina, Yugoslavia. He is a professional football player from Slovenia who currently plays forward for Maribor in the PrvaLiga. He began his playing career with the Triglav Kranj and then the Britof youth teams. He played for Interblock and Maribor in his native Slovenia after starting his professional career with the Slovenian club Bonifika.

On September 12, 2010, Ilicic made his Serie A debut for Palermo against Brescia, taking Giulio Migliaccio's place. In an exhibition match against Australia in August 2010, Iličić made his senior debut for the Slovenian national team. He scored his first goal for his country against Cyprus in September 2013, three years after making his debut. During his eleven-year career, he made 79 appearances for the squad overall. Ilicic signed a new five-year deal on June 20, 2011.

He signed with Fiorentina in 2013 and then, in 2017, with Atalanta. Atalanta bought Iličić from Fiorentina on July 5, 2017, reportedly paying €5.5 million for him. Ilicic went to Italy for a duration of twelve years before coming back to Maribor in 2022. In addition to being the oldest player to score four goals in a Champions League knockout game, he is also the first player to ever score four goals while playing away from home.
