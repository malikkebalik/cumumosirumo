Halloween is right around the corner, and we rounded up some of the best photos of Victoria’s Secret models in their best costumes over the years.

If there’s anything Victoria’s Secret models know, it is how to rock a killer costume both on and off the runway. Their special runway costumes are some of the most expensive, exuberant (and heavy) pieces out there. From the Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show runway to the streets of Los Angeles, there are quite a few lovely ladies who have been spotted strutting all their stuff off! 

For one day a year, the models do indeed ditch their glamorous wings and lingerie on the big stage for some quirky, inspiring Halloween costumes on a night out in the town (couples costumes included).
