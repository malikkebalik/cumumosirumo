Publish date: 2024-09-22

Who is Tamsin Egerton?

Tamsin Egerton is an actress as well as a model. Furthermore, she acted as Chelsea Parker in the movie St Trinian’s in 2007, Holly Goodfellow in the film Keeping Mum in 2005, and Guinevere in the TV series Camelot in 2011.

How old is Tamsin Egerton?

Tamsin Egerton was born on 26th November 1988 in Hampshire, UK. She is the daughter of father Michael J.Dick and mother Nicola Egerton Dick.

Her birth name is Tamsin Olivia Egerton-Dick and she is from the British community. She had a sister named Sophia Egerton with whom she had raised up. Besides this, there is no information about her childhood.

She had graduated from Ditcham Park School near Petersfield in Hampshire, where she says she was bullied. Then she pursued acting rather than going to the sixth form and then university.

Tamsin Egerton: Career, Awards, Net Worth

Regarding her professional life, she started her career in 2001. She had followed her elder sister Sophia Egerton into a local youth theatre when she was 6 years old.

She had also appeared in a Royal Shakespeare Company musical production of The Secret Garden, playing Mary. In 2006, she acted in the movie  Sarah in Driving Lessons alongside Rupert Grint and Flora in Knife Edge in 2008.

Earlier, she had also starred as the “young” Morgaine in the TV miniseries The Mists of Avalon in 2001. In the same year, she had appeared on stage – in the RSC’s musical version of The Secret Garden.

Subsequently, she started acting in the movie St Trinian’s sequel, St Trinian’s II: The Legend of Fritton’s Gold in 2009.

She had appeared in Chalet Girl alongside Felicity Jones, Ed Westwick, and Bill Nighy, playing “Georgie” in 2011. In the same year, she had even played  Guinevere in the US Starz adaptation of the Arthurian legend, Camelot.

Through her hard work in her professional life, she has earned a net worth of $275 million as of 2023. So far, she hasn’t achieved awards till now.
