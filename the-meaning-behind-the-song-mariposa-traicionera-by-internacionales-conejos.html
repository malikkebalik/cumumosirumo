The lyrics of Mariposa Traicionera tell a story of betrayal and heartbreak, reflecting the universal theme of love gone wrong. The song metaphorically compares the person who caused the pain to a deceptive butterfly, captivating and beautiful on the surface but ultimately leading to disappointment. The powerful and emotive delivery of the lyrics by the band’s lead vocalist adds to the emotional impact of the song.

Internacionales Conejos intended for Mariposa Traicionera to resonate with audiences who have experienced the pain of deceit in relationships. The lyrics serve as a reminder to be cautious and aware of the potential for betrayal, while also acknowledging the emotions and vulnerability that come with being in love. The song’s success can be attributed to its relatability and the band’s ability to evoke strong emotions through their music.
