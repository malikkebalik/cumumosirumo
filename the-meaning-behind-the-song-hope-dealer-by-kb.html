KB’s “Hope Dealer” is a song that focuses on the light found in the midst of darkness. It acknowledges the struggles and challenges that individuals face daily, emphasizing the importance of relying on hope to overcome them. The lyrics paint a vivid picture of the brokenness in the world, with KB himself acknowledging his own battles. However, he also offers a solution – hope.

The song encourages listeners to look beyond their current circumstances and find solace in the fact that hope is always available. KB believes that hope is not just a fleeting feeling but something tangible that can be found in God. He provides a refreshing perspective on struggles, suggesting that they can be transformed into opportunities to grow and strengthen one’s faith.
