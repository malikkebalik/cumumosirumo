Who is Alina Morse?

Alina Morse is an American teen entrepreneur and student. She is the CEO and founder of the multi-million-dollar company Zolli Candy.

Zollipops is the best-selling sugar-free, healthy candy in the United States.

Alina Morse is the youngest person who was on the cover of Entrepreneur Magazine in 2018.

Alina Morse: Age, Family, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Alina Morse was born in Wolverine Lake, Michigan, the US on May 8, 2005.  Her full name is Alina Starr Morse. As of 2022, she is 17 years old and her sun sign is Taurus.

She is the older daughter of Tom Morse and Suzanne Morse. Her father works as Alina’s manager and handles meetings whereas her mother worked as her coach, schedule organizer, and stylist.

Alina Morse: Profession Life, Career

At the age of seven, Alina started thinking about making a lollipop that would not rot teeth while eating candies. She started experiments in her home oven, stove, and microwave to make candies.

It took two years to make the formula through online research, collaboration with a food scientist, and her dentist on the ingredients.

Alina Morse invested $3,750 while settling on a formula and proposed their first retail placement to Whole Foods Market in 2014. In the first year, they sold Seventy thousand Zollipops and sales doubled each year from 2014 through 2018.

In 2016, Zollipops was picked by America’s largest supermarket chain, Kroger. Soon after, Zollipops were sold online via Amazon. In 2018, Zollipops was both Amazon’s best-selling sugar-free hard candy and best-selling lollipops.
