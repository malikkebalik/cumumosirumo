4 Zodiac Signs That Will Be Affected By The New Moon On January 11

Publish date: 2024-07-23

The first major lunar event of 2024 is upon us. On January 11, a new moon will rise in the sign of Capricorn and bring about an energy that sets the tone for the new year.

While it’s known that new moons signal new beginnings and growth, this particular moon is important as it will lay the groundwork for the months ahead.

This is the perfect time to usher in insight and clarity that would help you realise your vision for the future and the steps you might have to take to achieve it.

The new moon will also allow you to form sustainable lifestyle changes that are just what you need at the beginning of the new year.

While the lunar event will affect all zodiac signs in varying ways, four signs will face the impact of the new moon more than the others…

How The New Moon On January 11 Will Impact These 4 Zodiac Signs:

Gemini

This is the perfect time to release the emotional baggage you’ve been lugging around, Gemini. The new moon implores you to make deeper emotional connections by being unabashedly vulnerable and expressing your needs to your partner.

Leo

Leo, luck and abundance are within reach for you, but only if you revamp, reorganise and reset. Things might have appeared stagnant in many areas in your life and this is the perfect time to shake things up.

Libra

You’ll find yourself drawn to new things and opportunities, Libra. The new moon will light the need to transform but you must realise that the change has to come from within, before it manifests on the outside. A fresh attitude will open your eyes to exciting new opportunities.

Aquarius

Time for some serious soul-searching, Aquarius. The new moon invites you to lay a strong foundation for your future goals by seeking clarity from within. Your feet need to be firmly planted on the ground to allow your head to be in the clouds.
