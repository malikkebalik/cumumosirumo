Khar Ra is a Burmese actor, model, and singer who rose to acclaim after starring in the 2018 film Dimensions. Over the years, he has also been a part of several other projects such as City Hunters, Toxic Man, 74 Days of Love, It was on Yesterday 2, Lady Danger, Unstoppable, The Accident, Bella and Me, Spirit of Fight, and Lake Pyar.

Khar Ra Facts

  • He released his debut album titled Ka Lane Ka Kyait (Deceit) in 2016.
  • Alongside Alice Ong, Ja Seng Ing, Min Myat Soe San, and Win Myaing, Khar Ra co-starred in the 2018 action-thriller film Dimensions. This performance won him the Star Award in the “Best Rising Star (Male)” category.
  • He brought to life the character of Phoenix in the action sports television series Spirit of Fight from 2019 to 2020.
  • In 2020, Khar Ra started appearing in the fantasy mystery romantic drama television series 74 Days of Love.
  • He actively participated in the anti-coup movement following the 2021 Myanmar coup d’état.
