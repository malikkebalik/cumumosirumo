Who is David Lee Roth?

David Lee Roth is an American Rock Singer. David is best known for the soundtrack of Mission to Mars in 2000, Ready Player One in 2018, and Don’t Look Up in 2021.

Moreover, Lee is the writer of Cobra Kai (TV Series).

David Lee Roth: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, and Education

David was born on 10th October 1954 in Bloomington, Indiana, U.S. He is the son of Sibyl Roth and Nathan Lee Roth. His father is an Ophthalmologist meanwhile his mother is a teacher. Asides from that, he grew up with two sisters named, Lisa and Alison Roth.

Furthermore, Lee holds American nationality and belongs to the Caucasian ethnicity. Moving toward his educational background, Roth enrolled at The Webb Schools in Claremont, California. Later, he graduated from John Muir High School.

David Lee Roth: Career

David Lee Roth made his recording debut with the release of Eat ‘Em and Smile,  studio albums he co-wrote with the American former rock band Van Halen in 1986. Skyscraper, his second solo studio album, was released in 1988. A Little Ain’t Enough, his third studio album, was also released in 1991.

Additionally, David Lee Roth’s fourth studio album, Your Filthy Little Mouth, was released in 1994. His fifth studio album, DLR Band, was released in 1998. In 2003, he launched Diamond Dave, his sixth studio album. A Different Kind of Truth, the final album of Van Halen, was also released by Roth in 2012.

David Lee Roth: Awards and Nominations

  • He is the nominee of the VMA Awards for David Lee Roth: Just a Gigolo I Ain’t Got Nobody in 1985.
  • He is also the nominee of the VMA Awards for David Lee Roth: California Girls in 2009.
