RonaldOMG is a budding Canadian social media star who has managed to capture the attention of millions of fans around the globe with the content he uploads to his YouTube channel ronaldOMG. It features an incredible gaming experience based on highly anticipated RPG and FPS games like Minecraft, Roblox, and Fortnite. Furthermore, he is also known for the formation of the channel Sis Vs Bro alongside his older sister Karina Calor also known as GamerGirl. Together, they upload gaming, challenges, pranks, reviews, vlogs, and slime video content. Ronald also manages a 3rd YouTube account titled Ronald’s Life which mostly contains content based on his personal vlogs. With time, he has amassed a huge fan base with more than 5 million subscribers on his YouTube channel ronaldOMG, more than 800,000 subscribers on his channel Ronald’s life (later renamed to Ronald Kurzawa), and more than 1 million followers on Instagram.
