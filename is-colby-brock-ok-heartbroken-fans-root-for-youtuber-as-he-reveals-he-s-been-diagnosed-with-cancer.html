Who is Colby Brock?

Born on January 2, 1997, in Kansas, United States, Brock is a social media personality. He is known for the YouTube channel 'Sam and Colby' which he started with his friend Sam Golbach back on October 28, 2014, according to Wikitubia. The first video was uploaded on November 13, 2014. They started the channel with comedy and challenge videos but then diverted to post videos in the Overnight and Exploration series. However, on January 26, 2019, the duo was taken into custody for trespassing into an abandoned school that was under construction. On April 6, 2019, they updated that they would not make videos until further notice. On May 1, 2019, they announced their return.
