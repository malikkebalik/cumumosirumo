Simon Helberg Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-09-21

Who is Simon Helberg?

Simon Helberg is an American actor, comedian, and musician. He is best known for his role as Howard Wolowitz in the American TV sitcom The Big Bang Theory (2007-present).

He is also popular for his roles in the 2004 film A Cinderella Story and 2016 film Florence Foster Jenkins.

Simon Helberg: Birth Facts, Family, Childhood

Simon Helberg was born Simon Maxwell Helberg on 9 December 1980, in Los Angeles, California, USA. His nationality is American and he belongs to an Ashkenazi Jewish ethnicity.

He is the son of famous American actor Sandy Helberg and casting director, Harriet Helberg. He has a brother named Mason Helberg.

In which school did Simon Helberg attend?

Simon attended middle and high school at the Crossroads School in Santa Monica, California, with Jason Ritter, who later became his roommate at New York University.

He attended New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts.  He was also trained at the Atlantic Theater Company.

Simon Helberg: Professional Life and Career

Simon has been an active member of the industry since 1999. He made his acting debut with a small role in the movie Mumford (1999).

Since the early 2000s, he performed with comedian Derek Waters as the sketch comedy duo Derek & Simon.

He appeared in the 2002 feature film Van Wilder. He had a minor role in the 2003 movie Old School. In 2004, he was in two episodes of Reno 911!

Since then, he has played several minor roles in movies and TV films.  In 2007, he was cast as Howard Wolowitz in the American TV sitcom The Big Bang Theory, and he still portrays the role in the sitcom.

Simon has been nominated for several awards for his performance in the series. He voiced the character Bian Zao in Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness (2011-2016). He has a salary of $100 thousand/episode with The Big Bang Theory.
