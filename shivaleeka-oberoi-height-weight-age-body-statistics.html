Shivaleeka Oberoi is an Indian actor, model, and assistant director. She has been cast in movies such as Yeh Saali Aashiqui, Khuda Haafiz, and Khuda Haafiz: Chapter II – Agni Pariksha.

Education

Shivaleeka Oberoi studied at Arya Vidya Mandir School and Jamnabai Narsee School in Mumbai. After that, she graduated from the University of Mumbai (informally known as Mumbai University (MU), majoring in English and Psychology.

She has also done a 3-month diploma course from Anupam Kher‘s Actor Prepares acting institute.

    Shivaleeka Oberoi Facts

  • In 1967, her grandfather Mahavir Oberoi produced a Bollywood film titled Sheba and Hercules.
  • After graduating from Mumbai University, Shivaleeka Oberoi began working at Nadiadwala Grandson Entertainment and went on to serve as an assistant director on Kick (2014) and Housefull 3 (2016).
  • She made her first theatrical film appearance as Mitali “Mitee” Deora in the 2019 Hindi-language romantic thriller film Yeh Saali Aashiqui. She co-starred in the film opposite Vardhan Puri.
  • Alongside Vidyut Jammwal, Annu Kapoor, Shiv Panditt, and Aahana Kumra, Shivaleeka Oberoi co-starred as Nargis Rajput Choudhary (Sameer’s wife) in the 2020 Hindi-language direct-to-video action thriller film Khuda Haafiz.
  • She has also appeared in the music videos for songs like Choti Choti Galtiyan by Papon, Rehna Tere Paas by Armaan Malik, Saiyyonee by Yasser Desai and Rashmeet Kaur, and Soulmate by Akull and Aastha Gill.
