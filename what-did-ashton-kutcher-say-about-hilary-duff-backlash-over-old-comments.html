What did Ashton Kutcher say about Hilary Duff? Backlash Over Old Comments

Publish date: 2024-08-27

In a recent turn of events, actor, producer, entrepreneur, and former model Ashton Kutcher has found himself facing backlash for comments he made in 2003 during an episode of his reality TV show, “Punk’d.” These remarks resurfaced on social media, reigniting controversy.

During the episode in question, Kutcher made comments about a then-underage Hilary Duff, who was 15 at the time, saying, “She is one of the girls we are all waiting for to turn 18.” Similar comments were made about child actors Mary Kate and Ashley Olsen, known as the Olsen twins.

The controversy surrounding Kutcher deepened when it was revealed that he and his wife, actress Mila Kunis, had written letters of support for Danny Masterson, a former colleague from the television series “That ’70s Show.” Masterson was recently sentenced to 30 years in prison on two counts of sexual abuse.

Chrissie Carnell Bixler, one of Masterson’s accusers, brought this information to public attention through her Instagram Story, expressing her dismay at Kutcher and Kunis’s support for a convicted rapist.

“Punk’d” was a reality television series that centered around hidden-camera practical jokes. In its debut season, Kutcher played pranks on celebrities while their reactions were recorded on secret cameras. The show aired on MTV from 2003 to 2007.

During one of the episodes of “Punk’d,” Kutcher mentioned Hilary Duff, who was a child actor at the time, stating, “Hilary Duff is in Lizzie McGuire, and she also has an album out. She is going to be in a movie called Cheaper by the Dozen.” He then playfully commented that Duff was one of the girls everyone was eagerly anticipating turning 18, along with the Olsen twins.

Two decades later, the resurfacing of this old clip of Kutcher making controversial remarks about an underage Hilary Duff and the Olsen twins has sparked widespread outrage. Not only is Kutcher and his wife, Mila Kunis, facing criticism for their letters of support for Danny Masterson, but Ashton Kutcher is also being slammed for comments made in the past.

The fallout from this controversy has put Kutcher and Kunis in the hot seat, with netizens expressing their anger and disappointment over the actor’s two-decades-old comments and their support for Masterson in the wake of his conviction.
