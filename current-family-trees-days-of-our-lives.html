I think it’s important to remember that whatever source consulted to check out a family tree, or bio for a particular DOOL character, may contain errors and/or omissions. That usually happens because the writers tend to surprize us with new characters at will–often previously unknown relatives. It must be hard for whoever compiles these sources to keep the information current! I have found the soapcentral website and our own Character Listings generally accurate, but in both of them I’ve found minor inaccuracies or information that should be updated (especially the addition of “new” relatives).

There are always ongoing Spoilers revealing that certain newcomers are due in Salem in the near future. We cannot expect those names to be identified in a Family Tree until they actually arrive. And it may take a while before we see their names listed–as it did on two sources that I know of–with Victor’s previously unknown second brother and nephew “Xander.” (I believe there’s to be still another Kiriakis relative sprung on us soon.)
